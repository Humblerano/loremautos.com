Questions

Frequently Asked

Common inquiries about our financing programs

What credit score is required for approval?

While we work with clients across all credit profiles, our premier rates typically require a score of 700 or above. However, our relationships with specialized luxury lenders allow us to secure competitive financing for qualified buyers with scores as low as 650.

Can I finance a vehicle for export?

Yes, we offer specialized export financing for international clients. Our team handles all documentation, currency conversion, and logistics coordination. Terms vary by destination country and typically require a 30% down payment.

Are there prepayment penalties?

No. All Aurum financing agreements allow for early payoff without penalties. We believe in providing flexibility for our clients' changing financial situations.

What documentation is required?

Typically we require proof of income (tax returns or pay stubs), bank statements, proof of residence, and valid identification. For business entities, additional documentation such as articles of incorporation may be required.
